# Interview Notes - Notification Preferences
## Summary
This file captures synthesized findings from five user interviews conducted in Q1 focused on notification preferences and pain points. It documents recurring themes around notification fatigue, channel preferences, and desired controls. These insights directly informed the scope and prioritization decisions for Feature X - User Mention Alerts. This is an internal research artifact intended for the product team and LLM agents navigating product decisions.
*Tags:* #user_research #notifications #product #team_leads #v1

---

## Key Themes

### Notification Fatigue
- 4 of 5 users reported turning off all notifications from at least one app in the past month.
- Over-notification was cited as the primary reason for disabling alerts.

### Channel Preferences
- Email preferred for summaries; push preferred for time-sensitive events.
- In-app preferred when users are already active in the product.

### Desired Controls
- Users want per-channel toggles, not a single on/off switch.
- Several users requested a "quiet hours" feature.
