# Onboarding Playbook
## Summary
This playbook walks new team members through the first two weeks of onboarding, covering tool setup, access provisioning, key introductions, and initial project assignments. It is the primary reference for new hires and their managers during the onboarding period. It also documents which teams and systems require explicit access requests. The playbook is reviewed quarterly and updated when processes change.
*Tags:* #policy #onboarding #hr #employees #internal

---

## Week 1

### Day 1
- [ ] Receive laptop and log into SSO.
- [ ] Join Slack and introduce yourself in `#general`.
- [ ] Read [[Remote Work Policy]].
- [ ] Meet with your manager for a 1:1 kickoff.

### Day 2-5
- [ ] Complete security training (link in your onboarding email).
- [ ] Request access to GitHub, cloud console, and internal tools via IT ticket.
- [ ] Read [[Architecture Overview]] to get familiar with the system.
- [ ] Shadow at least one team standup or demo.

---

## Week 2

- [ ] Pick up your first task from the team backlog.
- [ ] Schedule 30-minute intros with 3 teammates outside your immediate team.
- [ ] Review one open pull request and leave at least one comment.
- [ ] Check in with your manager; share early blockers or questions.
