# Remote Work Policy
## Summary
This file documents the company's remote work policy including expectations for availability, communication, and equipment. It applies to all full-time employees and contractors working remotely, either fully or in a hybrid arrangement. It covers core hours, async communication norms, and expense reimbursement for home office equipment. This is the canonical policy reference for HR questions related to remote work.
*Tags:* #policy #hr #remote_work #employees #guidelines

---

## Core Hours

All team members are expected to be available and responsive between **10:00 AM - 3:00 PM in their local timezone**, Monday through Friday. Outside of core hours, async communication is the default.

---

## Communication Norms

- Default to async (Slack messages, comments, shared docs) over synchronous meetings.
- Respond to direct messages within 4 hours during core hours.
- Use Slack status to indicate focus time, meetings, or offline periods.
- Video on is encouraged but not required in meetings.
